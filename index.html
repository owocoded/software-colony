About the Programme

What is a Data Analyst?

A data analyst collects, processes, and interprets data to help organisations make informed decisions. They use tools like Excel, SQL, and visualisation software to uncover patterns and trends — turning raw data into actionable insights.

This programme is designed to take you from zero to confident in 8 weekends, with live instruction, real projects, and industry-standard tools.
